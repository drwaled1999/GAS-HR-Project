Account Deletion Request

GAS HR respects the privacy of its users and provides a process for requesting the deletion of an account and associated personal information.

How to Request Account Deletion

To request deletion of your GAS HR account, please contact the HR Administration using the email address below:

Email: bm9i9m@gmail.com

Information Required

Please include the following information in your request:

  • Full Name
  • Employee ID / GAS ID
  • Registered Username
  • Reason for the deletion request

Data That May Be Deleted

  • User account information
  • Profile information
  • Uploaded personal documents where applicable
  • Associated account access credentials

Data That May Be Retained

Certain employment, attendance, payroll, leave, audit, compliance, or legal records may be retained where required by company policies, operational requirements, or applicable laws.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are reviewed by GAS HR Administration and are typically processed within 30 days.

For any questions regarding account deletion or personal data, please contact:
