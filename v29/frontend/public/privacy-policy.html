GAS HR Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

GAS HR ("we", "our", or "the Application") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of employee information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, stored, and protected when using the GAS HR application.

Information We Collect

The application may collect and process the following information:

  • Full Name
  • Email Address
  • Phone Number
  • Employee ID / User ID
  • Department and Job Information
  • Attendance Records
  • Leave Requests and Related Information
  • Uploaded Documents and Attachments
  • Account Authentication Information

How We Use Information

The collected information is used for:

  • Managing employee accounts.
  • Attendance and timesheet management.
  • Leave and employee service requests.
  • Generating reports and HR records.
  • Security, compliance, and fraud prevention.
  • Providing application functionality and support.

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to protect user information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. All data is transmitted using secure encrypted connections.

Data Sharing

GAS HR does not sell user data. User information is not shared with third parties except when required by applicable laws, regulations, or legitimate business requirements.

Data Retention

Employee data may be retained as required for operational, legal, audit, and compliance purposes. Retention periods may vary depending on company policies and applicable regulations.
